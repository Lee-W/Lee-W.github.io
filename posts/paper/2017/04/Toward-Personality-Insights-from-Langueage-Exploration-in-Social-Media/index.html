[Paper] Toward Personality Insights from Language Exploration in Social Media

Posted on 2017/04/04 - 二 in Paper

Paper
Demo

My Slide

The main purpose of this paper is to show how show how social media can also be used to gain psychological insights.

Different from most researches in the past which use a pre-compiled word category list (e.g. LIWC), it uses an open vocabulary approach that allowing discovery of unanticipaated language.

Data

  • 75,000 Volunteers
    • Facebook Status Update
    • Age
    • Gender
    • Personality (Through Standard Personality Questionnaire)

Architecture

  1. Linguistic Feature Extraction

    • N-Gram
      • Point-Wise Mutual Inforamtion
    • Topic
      • Probability a person mentioning a topic (Derived from LDA)

  2. Correlation analysis

    • Least Squares Linear Regression
  3. Visualization
    • Differential Word Clouds
      • Word ize represents correlation strength.
      • Color represents relative frequency
    • Standardized Frequency Plot
      • Plot the word frequency against age
